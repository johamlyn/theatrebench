Therese Raquin

1860s Paris. In a small dusty haberdasher’s shop near the Seine in the dank, narrow Passage du Pont Neuf, the young and beautiful Thérèse Raquin is trapped into a loveless marriage to her sickly cousin, Camille.

'This exceptional political satire will be difficult to forget' The Upcoming

These Trees are Made of Blood

Set in a timeless Buenos Aires cabaret club before, during and after Argentina’s Dirty War, These Trees are Made of Blood tells the story of one Mother’s search for her daughter.

'This exceptional political satire will be difficult to forget' The Upcoming

These Trees are Made of Blood ran @ Southwark Playhouse | 18th March - 11th April 2015

Teddy

Teddy is a punchy, lyrical ride though the dark and damaged world of 1950s London. Featuring a blistering live performance from Johnny Valentine and The Broken Hearts and an electrifying original score by Dougal Irvine.

'Constantly compelling' The Guardian

Teddy ran @ Southwark Playhouse | 4th - 27th June 2015
